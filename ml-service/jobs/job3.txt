Develop basic Server Scripts (Python) and Client Scripts (JavaScript).

Support troubleshooting and enhancement of existing applications.
Assist with API integrations and data management tasks.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to improve business processes.
Participate in testing, documentation, and deployment activities.
